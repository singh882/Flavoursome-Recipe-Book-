Jalebi

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 25 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Sweet Jalebi Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water (for syrup)
  • Pinch of cardamom powder
  • Few drops of lemon juice
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a pan, combine 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water.
    • Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for about 10 minutes until it reaches a one-string consistency.
    • Add a pinch of cardamom powder and a few drops of lemon juice to prevent crystallization.
    • Remove from heat and keep warm.

  2. Prepare the Jalebi Batter:
    • In a bowl, mix 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons cornflour, and 1/2 teaspoon baking powder.
    • Add enough water to make a smooth batter (similar to pancake batter) and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  3. Heat the Oil:
    • In a deep frying pan, heat oil over medium heat.
    • To check if the oil is hot enough, drop a small amount of batter; it should sizzle and rise to the surface.

  4. Shape the Jalebi:
    • Pour the batter into a squeeze bottle or a piping bag with a round nozzle.
    • Once the oil is hot, squeeze the batter into the oil in a spiral shape, forming concentric circles.
    • Fry until golden and crisp on both sides, about 2-3 minutes.

  5. Coat with Sugar Syrup:
    • Remove the fried Jalebis from the oil and immediately dip them in the warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.
    • Remove and let excess syrup drip off.

  6. Serve:
    • Serve the Jalebis warm, garnished with chopped nuts if desired.

  7. Enjoy your delicious homemade Jalebi!
Happy cooking!
