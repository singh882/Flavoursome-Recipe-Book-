Garlic Avocado Mushroom

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms (sliced, e.g., button or cremini)
  • Avocado (diced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (e.g., parsley or cilantro, chopped, optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are tender and browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Remove from heat and gently stir in the diced avocado.
  6. Add lemon juice and fresh herbs, if using, and mix gently.
  7. Serve warm as a side dish or topping. Enjoy!
