Black Forest Cake

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Delicious, soft Black Forest Cake Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  1. Gather Ingredients:
    - For the cake:
       - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
       - 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
       - 2 cups granulated sugar
       - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
       - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
       - 1 teaspoon salt
       - 2 large eggs
       - 1 cup whole milk
       - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
       - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
       - 1 cup boiling water
    - For the filling and decoration:
       - 2 cups heavy whipping cream
       - 1/4 cup powdered sugar
       - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
       - 1 jar (about 24 oz) morello cherries (drained, reserve juice)
       - Chocolate shavings (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  • Mix Dry Ingredients:
    In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add Wet Ingredients:
    1. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
    2. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  • Pour Batter:
    Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  • Bake the Cakes:
    Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
    Let cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
  • Prepare Whipped Cream:
    1. In a large bowl, beat the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  • Assemble the Cake:
    1. Once the cakes are cool, slice each cake in half horizontally to create four layers.
    2. Place one layer on a serving plate, drizzle with reserved cherry juice, and spread a layer of whipped cream over it.
    3. Add a layer of cherries, then repeat with remaining layers.
    4. Top with the final cake layer and frost the top and sides with the remaining whipped cream.
    5. Garnish with chocolate shavings and additional cherries.
  • Chill and Serve:
    Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before slicing and serving.
    • Enjoy your delicious Black Forest Cake!
