Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • Palak (spinach) - 250 grams, blanched and pureed
  • Paneer - 250 grams, cubed
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1 medium, pureed
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Green chilies - 2, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Kashmiri red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - ½ teaspoon
  • Cream - ¼ cup (optional)
  • Cooking oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then cool and blend to a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a kadhai over medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for a minute.
  6. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  7. Add the spinach puree and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Add spices: Kashmiri red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and garam masala. Mix well.
  9. Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them in the gravy.
  10. If using, stir in the cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
