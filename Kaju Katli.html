Kaju Katli

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious diamond-shaped Kaju Katli Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cashew nuts
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Pinch of cardamom powder
  • Ghee (for greasing)
Enjoy making your Kaju Katli!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather 2 cups of cashew nuts, 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of water, and a pinch of cardamom powder.
    • Have some ghee ready for greasing.

  2. Soak the Cashews:
    • Soak the cashew nuts in water for about 2-3 hours.
    • Drain the cashews and let them dry slightly.

  3. Make Cashew Powder:
    • In a blender, grind the soaked cashews to a fine powder. Be careful not to over-blend, or it will turn into cashew butter.

  4. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a pan, combine 1 cup of sugar and 1/2 cup of water.
    • Bring it to a boil and let it simmer until it reaches a one-string consistency.

  5. Add Cashew Powder:
    • Once the syrup is ready, add the cashew powder and a pinch of cardamom powder.
    • Mix well and cook on low heat, stirring continuously until the mixture starts to leave the sides of the pan.

  6. Shape the Kaju Katli:
    • Grease a flat plate or tray with ghee.
    • Transfer the mixture to the greased surface and flatten it with a spatula.
    • Let it cool for a few minutes, then roll it out to your desired thickness.

  7. Cut and Serve:
    • Once cooled completely, cut the Kaju Katli into diamond or square shapes.
    • Serve and enjoy your delicious Kaju Katli!
Happy cooking!
