Rasgulla

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 30 MINS     COOK TIME 40 MINS    

Juicy Rasgulla Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice (or vinegar)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish (optional)
Enjoy making your Rasgullas!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chenna (Paneer):
    • Boil 1 liter of milk in a pan.
    • Once it comes to a boil, add lemon juice gradually until the milk curdles.
    • Remove from heat and strain the curds using a muslin cloth.
    • Rinse the curds under cold water to remove the acidic taste.
    • Squeeze out excess water and hang the cloth for about 30 minutes to drain completely.

  2. Knead the Chenna:
    • Transfer the drained Chenna to a bowl and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and soft.
    • Form small balls (about 1 inch in diameter) and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a large pot, dissolve 1 cup of sugar in 4 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
    • Add the prepared Chenna balls to the boiling syrup.
    • Cook for about 15-20 minutes, gently stirring occasionally, until the balls double in size.
    • Remove from heat and let them cool in the syrup.

  4. Serve the Rasgulla:
    • Transfer the cooled Rasgullas to a serving dish along with the syrup.
    • Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds (optional).
    • Serve chilled or at room temperature.

  5. Enjoy your delicious homemade Rasgullas!
