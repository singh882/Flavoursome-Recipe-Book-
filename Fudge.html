Fudge

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious Fudge Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and butter.

  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture comes to a boil.

  3. Continue boiling for 4-5 minutes without stirring, until the mixture reaches a soft-ball stage (about 234°F or 112°C).

  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  5. Add vanilla extract and nuts (if using) to the mixture.

  6. Beat the mixture with a wooden spoon until it thickens and loses its shine.

  7. Pour the fudge into a greased 8x8-inch baking dish and spread it evenly.

  8. Let it cool completely at room temperature, then cut into squares.

  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade fudge!
