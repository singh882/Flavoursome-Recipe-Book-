Banana Pudding

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious Banana Pudding Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 box (5 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3-4 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 box (12 oz) vanilla wafers
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the instant pudding mix and cold milk until smooth.

  2. Fold in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. In a serving dish, layer vanilla wafers on the bottom.

  4. Add a layer of sliced bananas over the wafers.

  5. Spread a layer of the pudding mixture over the bananas.

  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with the pudding mixture on top.

  7. Spread the whipped cream over the top layer of pudding.

  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

  9. Serve chilled and enjoy!
