Soya Chaap

Ingredients

  • 500g soya chaap (soaked in warm water)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Skewers (if grilling)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Add the soaked soya chaap to the marinade and coat well. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Thread the marinated soya chaap onto skewers.
  5. Brush with oil and grill for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with green chutney.
