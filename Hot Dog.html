Hot Dog

Ingredients

  • 2 hot dog buns
  • 2 sausages
  • Mustard and ketchup
  • Onion, chopped (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil or grill the sausages until cooked through.
  2. Place each sausage in a hot dog bun.
  3. Top with mustard, ketchup, chopped onions, and pickles as desired.
  4. Serve hot.
