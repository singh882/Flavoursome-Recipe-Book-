Gujiya

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 25 MINS     COOK TIME 50 MINS    

Sweet Favoury, Delicious Gujiya Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup khoya (dried milk) or ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ cup grated coconut (optional)
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour and ghee. Knead into a smooth dough using water as needed.

  2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  3. In a pan, heat the khoya until it melts. Stir in powdered sugar, grated coconut (if using), and cardamom powder. Mix well and let it cool.

  4. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a small circle (about 4-5 inches in diameter).

  5. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle.

  6. Fold the circle in half to create a half-moon shape. Seal the edges by pressing with your fingers or using a fork.

  7. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the gujiyas until golden brown on both sides.

  8. Remove and drain on paper towels to absorb excess oil.

  9. Allow to cool and serve. Enjoy your homemade gujiya!
