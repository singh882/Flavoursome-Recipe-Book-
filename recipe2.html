Matka Biryani

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Proper Tasty homemade Matka Biryani Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams mutton
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 1-2 sticks of cinnamon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/4 cup warm milk (for saffron)

Instructions

  1. Marinate mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and saut√© sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated mutton and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, green chilies, mint, and coriander leaves. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  5. Add whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf, cumin) and mix well.
  6. In a separate pot, boil water and cook the basmati rice until 70% done. Drain the rice.
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the mutton mixture.
  8. If using saffron, soak it in warm milk and drizzle over the rice.
  9. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for about 30-40 minutes.
  10. Once done, fluff the biryani gently and serve hot.
Enjoy your biryani!
