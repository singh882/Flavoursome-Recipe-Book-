Stuffed Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt.
  2. Add water to form a dough.
  3. Rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll into balls and cook.
