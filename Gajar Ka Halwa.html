Gajar Ka Halwa

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 25 MINS     COOK TIME 40 MINS    

tasty gajar ka halwa Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots (grated)
    (2.2 lbs carrots, grated)
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
    (4 cups full-fat milk)
  • 200 g sugar (adjust to taste)
    (1 cup sugar, adjust to taste)
  • 100 g ghee (clarified butter)
    (7 oz ghee)
  • 100 g khoya (mawa)
    (3.5 oz khoya)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
    (1/4 tsp cardamom powder)
  • A handful of nuts (almonds, cashews, and pistachios)
    (A handful of nuts)
  • Raisins (optional)
    (Raisins, optional)

Instructions

  1. Gather your ingredients:
    • 1 kg grated carrots
    • 1 liter full-fat milk
    • 200 grams sugar (adjust to taste)
    • 100 grams ghee (clarified butter)
    • 100 grams khoya (optional)
    • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios)
    • Cardamom powder (1 tsp)
  2. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the grated carrots and saut√© for about 5-7 minutes until they soften.
  4. Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until the milk reduces and thickens.
  6. Add sugar and mix well. Cook for another 10-15 minutes.
  7. If using khoya, crumble it and add it at this stage.
  8. Stir in the cardamom powder and mix well.
  9. Garnish with chopped nuts before serving.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature.
Enjoy making your delicious Gajar ka Halwa!
