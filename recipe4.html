Salmon Avacado Salad

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 10 MINS    

Proper Healthy salmon avacado salad Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • Fresh salmon fillet
  • Ripe avocado
  • Mixed salad greens (e.g., spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sesame seeds or nuts for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the salmon fillet with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or bake the salmon for about 6-8 minutes, or until cooked through.
  4. While the salmon is cooking, prepare the salad base by combining the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion in a large bowl.
  5. Once the salmon is cooked, let it cool slightly, then flake it into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh. Slice it into cubes.
  7. Add the flaked salmon and avocado to the salad bowl.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then toss gently to combine.
  9. Season with additional salt and pepper, if desired.
  10. Garnish with sesame seeds or nuts, if using, and serve immediately.
