Broccoli Ans Mushroom Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to brown.
  4. Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for an additional 5-7 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, stirring to coat the vegetables evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds if desired.
  8. Serve warm with rice or noodles.
