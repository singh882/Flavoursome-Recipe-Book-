Samosa

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 10 MINS    

Proper Tasty homemade Samosas Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Water (as needed)
  • For the filling:
    • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
    • 1/2 cup green peas (boiled)
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
    • 1 tablespoon ginger (grated)
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped, optional)
  • For frying:
    • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough:
    In a bowl, mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour with 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Add 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee and mix until crumbly. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the filling:
    In a pan, heat some oil. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them sizzle. Add 1 tablespoon of grated ginger, 1-2 chopped green chilies, and saut√© for a minute. Then, add 2 large boiled and mashed potatoes, 1/2 cup boiled green peas, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Optionally, add chopped fresh coriander leaves. Let it cool.
  3. Shape the samosas:
    Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval shape, cut it in half, and form a cone by folding one half. Seal the edge with a little water. Fill the cone with the potato mixture and seal the open edge by pressing it together.
  4. Fry the samosas:
    Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Once hot, gently add the samosas in batches. Fry until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve:
    Enjoy your samosas hot with chutney or sauce!
