Tandoori Chicken

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 30 MINS     COOK TIME 35 MINS    

Delicious , Luscious juicy Tandoori Chicken Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • Chicken (preferably leg quarters or whole chicken, cut into pieces)

  • Yogurt

  • Garlic (minced)

  • Ginger (grated)

  • Lemon juice

  • Vegetable oil

  • Tandoori masala (store-bought or homemade)

  • Turmeric powder

  • Red chili powder

  • Salt

  • Cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, minced garlic, grated ginger, lemon juice, and vegetable oil.
  2. Add tandoori masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well to form a marinade.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  4. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C) or prepare a grill.
  5. Place the marinated chicken on a baking sheet or grill, reserving any excess marinade.
  6. Bake or grill the chicken for about 25-30 minutes, basting with the reserved marinade halfway through.
  7. Check for doneness; the internal temperature should reach 165째F (75째C).
  8. Once cooked, remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
