Besan Ka Laddu

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious besan ka laddu Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) for garnishing
Enjoy making your Besan Ka Laddu!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather 2 cups of gram flour (besan), 1 cup of ghee, 1 cup of sugar, 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder, and chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) for garnishing.

  2. Roast the Besan:
    • In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat 1 cup of ghee on low heat.
    • Add 2 cups of gram flour (besan) and roast it, stirring continuously, until it turns golden brown and aromatic (about 10-15 minutes).

  3. Add Sugar and Cardamom:
    • Once the besan is roasted, remove it from heat and let it cool slightly.
    • Add 1 cup of sugar and 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder to the warm mixture and mix well until combined.

  4. Shape the Laddus:
    • Let the mixture cool enough to handle. Grease your hands with ghee.
    • Take small portions of the mixture and roll them into round laddus.

  5. Garnish and Serve:
    • Garnish each laddu with chopped nuts, pressing them lightly into the surface.
    • Let them cool completely before serving.

  6. Enjoy your delicious Besan Ka Laddu!
Happy cooking!
