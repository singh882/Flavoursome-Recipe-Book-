Pie

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Delicious Pie Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  1. Gather Ingredients:
    - 2 cups all-purpose flour
    - 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled
    - 1/2 cup sugar
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 cup cold water
    - Filling of your choice (e.g., apples, berries, or pumpkin)

Instructions

  • Make the Pie Crust:
    1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt.
    2. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    3. Gradually add cold water, mixing until the dough forms a ball.
    4. Divide the dough in half, shape into discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  • Prepare the Filling:
    - If using fruit, slice and mix with sugar and spices as desired.
    - If using a custard, prepare according to the recipe instructions.
  • Assemble the Pie:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Roll out one disc of dough and place it in a pie dish.
    3. Fill with the prepared filling.
    4. Roll out the second disc and place it over the filling, sealing the edges.
    5. Cut slits in the top crust for steam to escape.
  • Bake the Pie:
    1. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
    2. Let cool before serving.
