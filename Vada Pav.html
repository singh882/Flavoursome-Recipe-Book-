Vada Pav

Ingredients

  • For the Vada:
    • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
    • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
    • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying
  • For the Chutney:
    • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
    • 1-2 green chilies
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • Salt to taste
  • For Assembling:
    • 4 pav (Indian bread rolls)
    • Fried green chilies (optional)
    • Chaat masala (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter, then add green chilies and ginger paste.
  2. Add mashed potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Let it cool.
  3. Form the potato mixture into small balls (vadas).
  4. In a bowl, mix besan with water to form a thick batter. Dip each potato ball into the batter, coating it well.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying. Fry the coated vadas until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  6. For the chutney, blend coriander leaves, green chilies, lemon juice, and salt until smooth.
  7. To assemble, slice the pav horizontally and spread the chutney inside. Place a vada in between and add fried green chilies if desired.
  8. Sprinkle chaat masala on top, close the pav, and serve hot.
Enjoy your delicious Vada Pav!
