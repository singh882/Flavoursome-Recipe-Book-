Lime Mojito

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 1/2 cup soda water
  • Extra mint sprigs for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a glass, muddle the mint leaves and 1 lime wedge to release the mint oils.
  2. Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, then muddle again to release the lime juice.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the rum over the ice.
  5. Top with soda water and stir gently to combine.
  6. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge and a sprig of mint.
