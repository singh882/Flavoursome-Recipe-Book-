Mix Veg

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and water as needed. Cover and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or roti.
