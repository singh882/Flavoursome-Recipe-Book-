Biscoff Milk Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • 4-5 Biscoff cookies, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon Biscoff spread
  • Whipped cream for topping
  • Biscoff cookies for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, crushed Biscoff cookies, and Biscoff spread.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into glasses.
  4. Top with whipped cream and additional crushed Biscoff cookies.
  5. Garnish with whole Biscoff cookies if desired.
