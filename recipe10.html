Manchurian

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Tasty Manchurian Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • For the Manchurian Balls:
    • 1 cup grated cauliflower (or finely chopped)

    • 1/2 cup grated carrots

    • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper

    • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion

    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)

    • 1/4 cup cornflour

    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste

    • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)

    • Salt to taste

    • Oil (for frying)

  • For the Manchurian Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons oil

    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

    • 1 small onion (chopped)

    • 1/4 cup bell peppers (chopped)

    • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce

    • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)

    • 1 tablespoon vinegar

    • 1/2 cup vegetable stock or water

    • 1 teaspoon cornflour (mixed with water for thickening)

    • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine grated cauliflower, grated carrots, chopped bell pepper, chopped onion, all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Once hot, fry the Manchurian balls until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. For the sauce, heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Then add chopped onion and bell peppers, and sauté until they are slightly tender.
  6. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, and vegetable stock (or water). Stir well to combine.
  7. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then add the cornflour mixture to thicken. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.
  8. Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and gently toss to coat them evenly.
  9. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  10. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with rice or noodles.
