Vanilla and jelly Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla syrup
  • 1/4 cup jelly (your choice)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream and milk.
  2. Add the vanilla syrup and blend until smooth.
  3. Swirl in the jelly gently for a marbled effect.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
