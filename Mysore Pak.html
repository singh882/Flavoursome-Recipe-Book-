Mysore Pak

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Tasty Mysore Pak Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat the ghee on low flame.

  2. In another pan, mix sugar and water. Heat until the sugar dissolves and reaches a one-string consistency.

  3. Add gram flour to the ghee and stir continuously to avoid lumps.

  4. Once the flour is well mixed, slowly add the sugar syrup while stirring continuously.

  5. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.

  6. Pour the mixture into a greased plate and flatten it evenly.

  7. Allow it to cool and cut into pieces.
