Ghevar

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 25 MINS     COOK TIME 50 MINS    

Nice Ghevar Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cold water
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Pistachios (for garnish)
  • Almonds (for garnish)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and ghee. Mix until it resembles breadcrumbs.
  2. Gradually add cold water to form a smooth batter. The batter should be thin and pourable.
  3. Heat ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Once the ghee is hot, pour a ladleful of the batter into the center of the ghee.
  5. The batter will spread and form a honeycomb-like structure. Fry until golden brown.
  6. Remove the Ghevar and drain excess ghee on paper towels.
  7. In a separate pan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  8. Dip the fried Ghevar in the sugar syrup and let it soak for a few seconds.
  9. Garnish with cardamom powder, chopped pistachios, and almonds. Optionally, add saffron strands.
  10. Allow it to cool before serving.
