Modak

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

tasty Modak Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
    • 2 cups rice flour
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • For the filling:
    • 1 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
    • 1/2 cup jaggery (grated or powdered)
    • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) - optional
Enjoy making your Modaks!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the filling:
    • In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee over medium heat.
    • Add the grated coconut and saut√© for a couple of minutes.
    • Add jaggery and mix until it melts and combines with the coconut.
    • Stir in cardamom powder and any chopped nuts (if using). Cook for another 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat.

  2. Prepare the dough:
    • In a pot, bring 1 cup of water to a boil.
    • Add 2 cups of rice flour, 1 tablespoon of ghee, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
    • Mix well and cook on low heat until it forms a soft dough.
    • Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  3. Shape the Modaks:
    • Grease your hands with a little ghee.
    • Take a small portion of the dough and flatten it into a disc.
    • Add a spoonful of the coconut filling in the center.
    • Carefully fold the edges to form a cone, pinching at the top to seal.

  4. Steam the Modaks:
    • Place the Modaks in a steamer lined with banana leaves or a greased plate.
    • Steam for about 10-15 minutes until they become glossy.

  5. Serve:
    • Let the Modaks cool slightly before serving.
    • Enjoy your delicious Modaks with a drizzle of ghee on top!
