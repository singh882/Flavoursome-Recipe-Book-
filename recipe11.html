Egg Bhurji

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 10 MINS     COOK TIME 15 MINS    

Egg Bhurji Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon garam masala

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Stir in the green chilies and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  5. In a bowl, beat the eggs until well mixed.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and stir continuously to scramble them with the onion-tomato mixture.
  7. Cook until the eggs are fully cooked but still soft and fluffy (about 3-4 minutes). If using, add garam masala at this stage and mix well.
  8. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with bread, chapati, or paratha.
