Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh coriander and lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Add rice flour, cornflour, and egg to the marinated chicken, mixing well to coat.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  4. Fry the marinated chicken pieces in batches until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with lemon wedges.
