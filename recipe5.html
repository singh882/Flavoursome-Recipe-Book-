Chhola Bhatura

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 30 MINS     COOK TIME 35 MINS    

Delicious Chhola bhatura Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

  • Chickpeas: 1 cup (soaked overnight)
  • Onion: 1 large (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium (pureed)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Green Chilies: 2 (slit)
  • Chole Masala: 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam Masala: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh Coriander: for garnish
Bhature (Fried Bread)
  • All-Purpose Flour (Maida): 2 cups
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Baking Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Warm Water: as needed (for kneading)
  • Oil: for deep frying
Enjoy cooking!

Instructions

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

  1. Soak 1 cup of chickpeas overnight.
  2. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil and add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds.
  3. Add 1 large finely chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and 2 slit green chilies; cook for a minute.
  5. Add 2 medium pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Stir in 1 tablespoon of chole masala, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, and salt to taste.
  7. Add the soaked chickpeas along with 2 cups of water; bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until the chickpeas are tender.
  9. Finish with 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
Bhature (Fried Bread)
  1. In a bowl, mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of yogurt, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  2. Knead into a soft dough using warm water as needed.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 2 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a circle.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry each rolled bhature until golden and puffed.
Enjoy your cooking!
