Kheer

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 10 MINS     COOK TIME 13 MINS    

Sweet tasty Kheer Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup nuts (almonds, raisins, cashews, pistachios)
  • Raisins (to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • Pinch of saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat ghee and add the rinsed rice. Saut√© for a couple of minutes until the rice is slightly translucent.
  3. Add the milk to the pan and bring it to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until the rice is cooked and the mixture thickens (about 20-25 minutes).
  5. Stir in the sugar and condensed milk (if using), and cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  6. Add the cardamom powder, nuts, and saffron (if using). Mix well.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Kheer can be served warm or chilled.
