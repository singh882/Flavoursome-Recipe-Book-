Boondi Ka Laddu

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious boondi ka laddu Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Handful of chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) for garnishing
  • Ghee (for frying and greasing)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather 1 cup gram flour (besan), 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder, and a handful of chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) for garnishing.
    • Have some ghee ready for frying.

  2. Make the Batter:
    • In a mixing bowl, add 1 cup of gram flour and gradually mix in water to form a smooth batter. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.

  3. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a pan, combine 1 cup of sugar and 1/2 cup of water.
    • Bring to a boil and let it simmer until it reaches a one-string consistency.
    • Add 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder to the syrup and mix well.

  4. Fry the Boondi:
    • Heat ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
    • Using a perforated ladle, pour the batter into the hot ghee, allowing it to form small droplets (boondi).
    • Fry until they turn golden brown, then remove and drain on paper towels.

  5. Combine Boondi with Sugar Syrup:
    • Once all the boondi is fried, add it to the warm sugar syrup.
    • Mix well to ensure all boondi is coated with syrup.

  6. Shape the Laddus:
    • Let the mixture cool slightly until manageable.
    • Grease your hands with ghee and take small portions of the mixture to form round laddus.

  7. Garnish and Serve:
    • Garnish with chopped nuts on top of the laddus.
    • Let them cool completely before serving.

  8. Enjoy your delicious Boondi Ka Laddu!
Happy cooking!
