Blue Lagoon Mocktail

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup blue curaçao syrup (non-alcoholic)
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 1/2 cup soda water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices for garnish
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a glass, add ice cubes.
  2. Pour the blue curaçao syrup over the ice.
  3. Add the lemonade and stir gently.
  4. Top with soda water and stir lightly to combine.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves.
