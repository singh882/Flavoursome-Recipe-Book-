Tandoori Chilli Paneer

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 10 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Proper Tasty homemade Chilli Paneer Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is to make our most popular vegetarian Indian-ish beauty!

Ingredients

200gms Paneer/ Indian cottage cheese chopped into cubes
Oil for frying
for the batter;
2 tbsp rice flour
2 heaped tbsp corn flour
1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder or mild paprika
½ tsp white pepper powder
½ tsp garlic paste
Salt to taste
80mls water
for the sauce;
3 tbsp vegetable oil
2 tsp garlic finely chopped
1 tsp ginger finely chopped
80gms red onion chopped into cubes
1 medium red or green pepper chopped into cubes
1 green chilli slit lengthwise
2 tbsp shop bought chilli garlic sauce
1 tsp dark soy sauce or kecap manis
1 tsp honey
Salt to taste
1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
Ginger matchsticks and chopped chilli for garnish

Instructions

1.Add cornflour, kashmiri chilli powder, salt to a bowl and give it a whisk.


2. the paneer cubes and toss to coat. Sprinkle a little water just to get the coating to stick to the paneer. Toss and set aside. Heat oil in a wok or pan and add the paneer piece by piece to prevent them from sticking to each other. Fry them on high heat for 2-3 minutes, turning once or twice till they are golden brown. Take them out on a plate and set aside. Whisk together all the ingredients under sauce and set aside.


3.Remove any excess oil from the wok, leaving behind 1.5 tbsp oil. Once the oil is hot, add ginger, garlic and green chillies. Saute for a minute or two and add onions and capsicum. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes on high heat and add the paneer cubes. Toss the paneer with the aromatics for a minute. Give the sauce a whisk and add it to the wok. Toss together on high heat for 2 minutes till the cornflour is cooked and the sauce takes on a thick, glossy finish and coats the paneer pieces. Add a little water if you want a little more sauce. Sprinkle with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
