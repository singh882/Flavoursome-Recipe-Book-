Pav Bhaji

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 30 MINS    

Delicious , Buttery pav bhaji Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Pav (bread rolls)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
  • Chopped onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan.

  2. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.

  3. Mix in 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.

  4. Add 1 chopped tomato and cook until soft.

  5. Add 1 cup of boiled and mashed mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, and bell peppers).

  6. Stir in 2 tablespoons of Pav Bhaji masala and salt to taste.

  7. Add a little water to adjust the consistency and cook for 5-7 minutes.

  8. In another pan, toast pav (bread rolls) with butter until golden brown.

  9. Serve hot bhaji with toasted pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.
