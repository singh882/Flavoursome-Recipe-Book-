Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 patties (vegetable or meat)
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Ketchup and mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Cook patties on a grill or in a pan until golden brown.
  2. Toast burger buns lightly on the grill.
  3. Assemble the burger: bottom bun, lettuce, patty, tomato, onion, cheese, ketchup, and mayonnaise.
  4. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.
