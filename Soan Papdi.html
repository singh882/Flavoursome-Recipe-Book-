Soan Papdi

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 40 MINS    

Delicious thin crust soan papdi Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Pinch of cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnishing
Enjoy making your Soan Papdi!

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather 2 cups of all-purpose flour (maida), 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of ghee, 1/2 cup of water, and a pinch of cardamom powder.
    • Chop nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnishing.

  2. Make the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a pan, combine 1 cup of sugar and 1/2 cup of water.
    • Heat until the sugar dissolves and the mixture reaches a one-string consistency.
    • Add a pinch of cardamom powder and mix well.

  3. Prepare the Flour Mixture:
    • In a separate pan, heat 1/2 cup of ghee.
    • Add 2 cups of all-purpose flour and roast it on low heat until it turns golden brown and aromatic (about 10-12 minutes).

  4. Mix the Flour and Syrup:
    • Once the flour is roasted, remove it from heat and let it cool slightly.
    • Gradually pour the sugar syrup into the roasted flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
    • Mix well until it forms a smooth batter.

  5. Stretch the Mixture:
    • Allow the mixture to cool slightly, then grease your hands with ghee.
    • Take small portions of the mixture and stretch it between your hands to form thin, long strands.
    • Fold these strands to create layers and shape them into a square or rectangle.

  6. Set and Cut:
    • Let the Soan Papdi cool completely at room temperature.
    • Once set, cut into squares or diamonds.

  7. Serve:
    • Garnish with chopped nuts and enjoy your delicious Soan Papdi!
