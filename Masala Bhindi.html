Masala Bhindi

Ingredients

  • Bhindi (okra) - 250 grams, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Green chilies - 2, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - ½ teaspoon
  • Kashmiri red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - ½ teaspoon
  • Cooking oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a kadhai over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add spices: turmeric powder, Kashmiri red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the cut bhindi and stir gently to coat with the spices.
  8. Cover and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until bhindi is tender.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
Enjoy cooking these delicious dishes!
