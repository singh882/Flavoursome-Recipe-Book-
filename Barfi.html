Barfi

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 15 MINS     COOK TIME 20 MINS    

Delicious barfi Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated khoya (or milk powder)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnishing
  • Ghee (for greasing)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Gather 2 cups of grated khoya (or milk powder), 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of milk, and 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder.
    • Chop nuts like almonds and pistachios for garnishing.

  2. Cook the Mixture:
    • In a heavy-bottomed pan, add the grated khoya and 1/2 cup of milk.
    • Cook on low heat, stirring continuously to avoid burning, until the mixture thickens.

  3. Add Sugar and Cardamom:
    • Once the mixture thickens, add 1 cup of sugar and 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder.
    • Continue to cook, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely and the mixture leaves the sides of the pan.

  4. Set the Barfi:
    • Grease a plate or tray with ghee.
    • Pour the mixture into the greased plate and spread it evenly with a spatula.
    • Garnish with chopped nuts, pressing them lightly into the surface.

  5. Cool and Cut:
    • Allow the Barfi to cool at room temperature for a few hours until set.
    • Once set, cut into square or diamond shapes.

  6. Serve:
    • Enjoy your delicious Barfi! Store any leftovers in an airtight container.
Happy cooking!
