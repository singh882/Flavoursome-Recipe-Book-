Gulab Jamun

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 20 MINS     COOK TIME 10 MINS    

Sweet, Delicious Gulab Jamun Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • For the Gulab Jamun Balls:
    • 1 cup khoya (mawa)

    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)

    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

    • 2 tablespoons milk (or as needed)

    • Oil or ghee (for frying)
  • For the Sugar Syrup:
    • 1 cup sugar

    • 1 cup water

    • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

    • 1/2 teaspoon rose water (optional)

    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (to prevent crystallization)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine khoya, all-purpose flour, and baking powder. Mix well until smooth.
  2. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough. Ensure the dough is not too sticky or too dry.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls, making sure there are no cracks.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over low to medium heat for frying.
  5. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the rolled balls. Fry them gently, stirring continuously to ensure even cooking and browning.
  6. Fry until they turn golden brown. Remove the balls from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. For the sugar syrup, combine sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then add cardamom powder and lemon juice.
  8. Simmer the syrup for 5-7 minutes until slightly thickened. Add rose water if using.
  9. Once the syrup is ready, add the fried Gulab Jamun balls to the syrup and let them soak for at least 30 minutes.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature, garnished with chopped nuts if desired.
