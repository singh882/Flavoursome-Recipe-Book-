Chocolate Brownies

SERVINGS 3/4    PREP TIME 25 MINS     COOK TIME 50 MINS    

Chocolaty Brownie Recipe, quick & easy to knock together in minutes and has everyone salivating every time. Give it a go and see for yourself how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225 grams) unsalted butter

  • 2 cups (400 grams) granulated sugar

  • 4 large eggs

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 cup (125 grams) all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup (90 grams) unsweetened cocoa powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

  • 1 cup (175 grams) chocolate chips (optional)

Enjoy baking your brownies!

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  3. In a saucepan, melt 1 cup of unsalted butter over low heat.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in 2 cups of granulated sugar until well combined.
  5. Add 4 large eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  7. Sift together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder in a separate bowl.
  8. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.
  9. If using, fold in 1 cup of chocolate chips.
  10. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  12. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting them into squares.
  13. Enjoy your delicious chocolate brownies!
Happy baking!
