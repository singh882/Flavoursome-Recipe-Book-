Pineapple Passionfruit Popper

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup passionfruit pulp (fresh or canned)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cup soda water or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Pineapple wedges for garnish
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a shaker, combine pineapple juice, passionfruit pulp, and lime juice.
  2. Add ice cubes and shake well.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with soda water or sparkling water.
  5. Garnish with pineapple wedges and mint leaves if desired.
