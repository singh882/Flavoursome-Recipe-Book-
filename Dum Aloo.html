Dum Aloo

Ingredients

  • 500g baby potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the baby potatoes until tender, then peel and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds until they splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Add the yogurt and mix well.
  7. Add the boiled potatoes and cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
Enjoy your cooking!
