Keto Chicken Cobb Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce (e.g., romaine or mixed greens)
  • Cooked chicken breast (diced or shredded)
  • Bacon (cooked and crumbled)
  • Avocado (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (diced)
  • Hard-boiled eggs (chopped)
  • Blue cheese (crumbled)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, slice the avocado, and chop the hard-boiled eggs.
  2. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy, then crumble it into small pieces.
  3. In a large bowl or platter, layer the lettuce as the base.
  4. Scatter the diced or shredded chicken over the lettuce.
  5. Distribute the bacon, avocado, tomatoes, hard-boiled eggs, blue cheese, and red onion evenly over the salad.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving, and toss gently if desired. Enjoy!
